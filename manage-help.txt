
You can find list of chats in chats.txt

h|help - print help
ls - print groups
new|n|create - create group. params: [aliases]
aa|add-aliases - add aliases to group. params: group alias, [new aliases]
am|add|add-member - add member to group. params: group alias [members]. member: (part of name|username)|id
rm|remove|remove-members - remove members from group. params: group alias [members]. member: (part of name|username)|id
